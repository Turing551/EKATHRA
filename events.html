ROUND 2:
  • Time of flight of the rocket will be considered. (Participants can add parachute to their rockets).
  • NOTE: - Adding of parachute is not mandatory, rockets without parachutes also will be allowed to the round 2.

  • This round is target hitting. Circles will be drawn on the ground, 70 metres away from the launching point. The team has to hit target to get points.
  • NOTE: - There will be circles drawn of different diameters. So, Inner circle has higher points and Outer circles have less point than Inner.
EVENT PROCEDURE
  • Every team should participate in round 1 and 2.
  • [Round 1 (Range in meters) + (10*Time of flight in seconds)] is calculated and top are qualified to round 3.
  • E.g.:- 50 meters range in first round and 8 sec of time of flight in second round. Then, Points calculation will be like this [50+ (10*8)]= 130 points
