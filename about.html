About EKATHRA

EKATHRA, is the annual technical and cultural festival of GVPCOE(A). Held towards the beginning of March every year, it attracts a whopping crowd of over 10,000 students from more than 100 colleges statewide. EKATHRA is devoted to creating an unforgettable experience for all its visitors, simultaneously providing unique opportunities to the youth for showcasing their talents in front of extremely large audiences and top-notch judges. Being a massive platform for providing technical knowledge and cultural exchange, Ekathra 2018 is ready to take all its visitors on a delightful ride. The 3rd edition of the festival will be held from the 8th March to 9th March, 2018. Join us for a glorious carnival!

With a plethora of events encompassing a wide variety of fields like Automation and Robotics, Construction and Design, Software and Electronics, Management and many more, EKATHRA attracts some of the brightest and most creative young minds from all over India. Various workshops are also organized every year, under expert guidance, to keep students up to date with latest technology.

Other major attractions for audiences have been the proshows like Dances, Musical events and comedy shows, which have always been crowd pullers. Acts like Doomsday, Rock Band, Photography events and many more. EKATHRA also conducts social initiatives year round aiming to bring the rich technical knowledge of the students and infrastructure at their disposal to benefit the less fortunate. These social initiatives are done with the assistance of organizations like ROTRACT, NATIONAL SERVICE SCHEME, etc.
